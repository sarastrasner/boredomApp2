# Feature Tasks
1. Groups
  - Users can create a group
  - Users can join an existing group
  - lists are shared/updated among the entire group
  - Group members can upvote and downvote list items in their group
2. Shuffle Feature
  - A user can shuffle a list, grabbing a random item
  - Items with higher rating appear more often in the shuffled results
3. Items Priority
  - when adding an item to the list, the user can give it a starting rating out of 10 based on how badly they want to watch that particular item 
  - other users within a group can upvode or downvote an item, affecting its priority
